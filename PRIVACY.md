## 1. Overview

NorthStar Holdings Trust operates the NorthStar LLM API. This policy explains how we handle information in connection with the Service. We are committed to the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles.

## 2. Information We Collect

**Account information:**
- Name, email address, and company name provided when requesting API access

**Operational logs:**
- Timestamp of each API request
- Client identifier (API key reference)
- Endpoint accessed
- HTTP status code returned

**We do not collect or store:**
- The content of prompts sent to the API
- The content of responses returned by the API
- Any personal or sensitive information contained within prompts

## 3. How We Use Information

Account information is used to issue and manage API keys, send service communications, and process billing via Stripe.

Operational logs are used to monitor service health, detect abuse, and generate usage reports.

## 4. Data Location

All data is stored and processed on infrastructure located in South Australia, Australia. No data is transmitted to or stored on servers outside Australia.

## 5. Third Party Services

**Stripe** — Payment processing. Stripe's privacy policy applies to billing data.

**Cloudflare** — Network infrastructure. Traffic passes through Cloudflare's Australian edge nodes in Sydney and Melbourne.

## 6. Data Retention

Operational logs are retained for 90 days then deleted. Account information is retained for the duration of the client relationship plus 7 years for legal compliance.

## 7. Your Rights

Under the Australian Privacy Act you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. Contact: northstarholdings.global@gmail.com

For unresolved privacy complaints contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner at www.oaic.gov.au
