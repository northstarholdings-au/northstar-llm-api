How It Works
Structured evidence in
four steps

Submit your evidence claims. PAIR-X analyses, grades, and structures them into two outputs — one for human review, one for AI agent evaluation.

01

Submit evidence claims

Provide up to 10 evidence claims with supporting documentation descriptions. Revenue, operational, legal, IP, or market evidence — any verifiable claim.

02

PAIR-X conformance analysis

Evidence Categories
Every evidence type.
One structured framework.

PAIR-X handles all categories of PE diligence evidence — financial, operational, legal, IP, and market. Each category is graded against a defined conformance standard.

CATEGORY 01

Financial Evidence

Revenue, EBITDA, margin, cash flow, debt structure, audit history. PAIR-X grades completeness, period coverage, audit status, and verification pathway.

CATEGORY 02

Operational Evidence

Uptime records, delivery metrics, customer retention, team structure, key person dependencies. Operational claims are graded for specificity and verifiability.

CATEGORY 03

Legal & Compliance Evidence

Litigation history, IP ownership, regulatory compliance, contract enforceability. Legal evidence is graded for jurisdiction, currency, and verification source quality.

CATEGORY 04

Market & Commercial Evidence

Market size claims, competitive position, customer concentration, pipeline quality, partnership agreements. Commercial claims graded for specificity and third-party validation.
