capability architecture.

Each engine addresses a distinct dimension of organisational capability. Together they produce a complete, defensible assessment that no single practitioner can replicate.

Assesses the organisation's capacity to think, plan, and execute at altitude. Strategic clarity, horizon scanning, and decision architecture under uncertainty.

M8
