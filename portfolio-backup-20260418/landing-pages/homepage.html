About the Trust
Structure, purpose,
and intent

What NorthStar Holdings Trust is

A discretionary trust registered in South Australia, holding a portfolio of AI and decision-architecture IP assets. The Trust operates as both a commercialisation vehicle and a direct service provider, with assets at various stages of deployment and licensing readiness.

How the portfolio is structured

Each asset is maintained as independent IP — separately documented, separately valued, and separately commercialisable. Assets can be licensed individually, acquired as a bundle, or integrated into existing enterprise frameworks. No asset is dependent on another.

Commercial model

Revenue pathways include direct subscription (NorthStar LLM API), methodology licensing (iLOGIX, Quadrant, TCC), evidence infrastructure licensing (PAIR-X), and content subscription (Publicious). The Trust is structured to support both recurring revenue and exit events.
